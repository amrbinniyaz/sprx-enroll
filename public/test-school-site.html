Welcome to Alleyn's

Alleyn's is one of the UK's leading co-educational independent schools, set on a stunning 30-acre campus in the heart of Dulwich, South London. For over 400 years, we have been inspiring young people to discover their passions, develop their talents, and make a positive difference in the world.

Our approach to education is holistic. We combine academic rigour with an exceptional co-curricular programme that spans sport, drama, music, art, and community service. Every pupil is known, valued, and supported to achieve their personal best.

We pride ourselves on our warm, inclusive community where pupils from all backgrounds come together to learn, grow, and thrive. Our diverse intake and generous bursary programme ensure that an Alleyn's education is open to all who would benefit from it.

Why Choose Alleyn's?

Academic Excellence

Consistently outstanding results at GCSE and A Level, with leavers securing places at the best universities in the UK and worldwide, including Oxford, Cambridge, and Ivy League institutions.

Outstanding Facilities

Our 30-acre campus includes a state-of-the-art science centre, performing arts complex, Olympic-standard swimming pool, and all-weather sports pitches.

Pastoral Care

Every pupil is assigned a personal tutor who oversees their academic progress and wellbeing. Our dedicated pastoral team ensures every child feels safe, supported, and happy.

Co-Curricular Programme

Over 100 clubs and societies, from robotics to debating, fencing to filmmaking. We believe education extends far beyond the classroom.

Our History

Alleyn's School was founded in 1619 by Edward Alleyn, the Elizabethan actor and entrepreneur. Originally part of the College of God's Gift, the school has grown from a small local foundation to one of the most respected independent schools in the country.

Throughout our history, we have remained true to our founder's vision of providing an education that combines intellectual rigour with compassion and service. Our alumni include Nobel laureates, Olympic athletes, award-winning artists, and leaders in every field of human endeavour.

Today, we continue to build on this proud heritage while embracing innovation and looking to the future. Our recent developments include a new Sixth Form centre, a cutting-edge STEM facility, and an expanded bursary programme that supports over 15% of our pupils.
