What to Expect

Our Open Days are designed to give you a genuine insight into life at Alleyn's. Here is a typical Open Day schedule.

9:15 am

Arrival & Registration

Arrive at the main entrance and collect your visitor badge and programme. Refreshments are served in the Great Hall.

9:45 am

Welcome Talk

The Headmaster gives a welcome talk covering the school's ethos, academic approach, and admissions process.

10:15 am

Guided Campus Tour

Sixth Form students lead small group tours of the campus, including classrooms, science labs, sports facilities, theatre, and art studios.

11:00 am

Lesson Observations

Observe live lessons across a range of subjects. See our teaching in action and get a feel for the learning environment.

11:30 am

Subject Fair

Meet department heads and teachers at our subject fair. Ask questions, see work on display, and explore our curriculum.

12:00 pm

Q&A Panel

A panel of current parents, pupils, and teachers answer your questions about life at Alleyn's.

12:30 pm

Informal Refreshments

Enjoy refreshments and continue conversations with staff and current families in a relaxed setting.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable shoes for walking around our 30-acre campus
  • A notepad and pen for any questions or observations
  • Your child is very welcome to attend with you
  • An umbrella (just in case — this is London!)
  • Any specific questions about admissions, bursaries, or school life

Getting Here

Alleyn's School is located on Townley Road, Dulwich, London SE22 8SU. We are a 10-minute walk from North Dulwich station (Southern Rail) and well served by buses (P4, 37, 40, 176, 185).

Limited on-site parking is available for Open Day visitors. We recommend using public transport where possible. There is also pay-and-display parking on surrounding streets.

Can't Make an Open Day?

We understand that not everyone can attend a scheduled Open Day. We are happy to arrange individual visits and tours by appointment on school days. Please contact our admissions office to arrange a personal visit.

Private Tours

If you are unable to attend one of our scheduled Open Days, we are delighted to offer private tours by appointment. These typically last around 90 minutes and include a meeting with a member of the Senior Leadership Team.

Private tours are available on most school days during term time, subject to availability. We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance.

Open Day FAQs

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, all Open Days must be booked in advance. Places are limited and events often fill up quickly, so we recommend booking as early as possible.

Can I bring my child?

Absolutely. We encourage you to bring your child along. They will have the chance to meet current pupils, see the facilities, and get a feel for the school.

Is there wheelchair access?

Yes, our campus is fully accessible. Please let us know in advance if you have any specific access requirements so we can ensure your visit is comfortable.

How long does an Open Day last?

Open Days typically run from 9:15am to 12:30pm (approximately 3 hours). You are welcome to leave earlier if you need to, but we recommend staying for the full programme.

Can I speak to the Headmaster?

The Headmaster attends all Open Days and is available for informal conversations during the refreshments period. For a more in-depth discussion, you may request a private meeting through the admissions office.
