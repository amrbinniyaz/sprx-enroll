School Life

Education at Alleyn's extends far beyond the classroom. Discover the rich co-curricular life that makes our school unique.

Beyond the Classroom

At Alleyn's, we believe passionately that a great education is about more than academic results. Our extraordinary co-curricular programme offers over 100 clubs, societies, and activities, ensuring every pupil can discover and develop their unique talents and interests.

From the sports field to the stage, from the science lab to the art studio, Alleyn's pupils have the freedom to explore, experiment, and excel. Our dedicated staff give generously of their time to run activities before school, during lunchtimes, after school, and at weekends.

We are proud that the vast majority of our pupils participate in at least two co-curricular activities each week, and many are involved in far more. It is this breadth of experience that helps our pupils develop into confident, well-rounded individuals.

Sport

Sport plays a central role in life at Alleyn's. Our 30-acre campus provides outstanding facilities, and we field competitive teams in a wide range of sports at local, regional, and national level.

⚽

Football & Rugby

Boys' and girls' teams compete at county level. Full-size pitches and floodlit training areas.

🏊

Swimming

25m indoor pool with dedicated coaching. Competitive galas and learn-to-swim programmes.

🎾

Cricket & Tennis

Six tennis courts and a cricket square. Summer term fixtures against leading schools.

🏃

Athletics & Cross Country

Full athletics track and cross country course through the school grounds and Dulwich Park.

Other Sports

We also offer netball, hockey, basketball, badminton, fencing, rowing, water polo, and table tennis. Our sports scholars benefit from specialist coaching and strength and conditioning programmes.

Drama & Theatre

Alleyn's has a long and distinguished tradition in drama, fitting for a school founded by the great Elizabethan actor Edward Alleyn. Our performing arts programme is one of the most vibrant in the country.

🎭

Major Productions

Two major productions each year in our 400-seat Edward Alleyn Theatre, from Shakespeare to contemporary drama.

🎤

House Drama

Every house stages a play in the annual House Drama competition, with pupils directing and producing.

Our purpose-built Edward Alleyn Theatre seats 400 and is equipped with professional lighting and sound. We also have a studio theatre for smaller, more experimental work. Drama is taught as a timetabled subject through to GCSE and A Level, and many of our alumni have gone on to careers on stage and screen.

"The drama department at Alleyn's gave me the confidence to pursue acting professionally. The facilities are world-class and the teaching is truly inspiring."

— Former pupil, now West End performer

Pastoral Care

The wellbeing of every pupil is our highest priority. Our pastoral care system is designed to ensure that every child feels known, valued, safe, and happy at Alleyn's.

The House System

Every pupil belongs to one of our eight houses, each led by a Head of House and supported by a team of house tutors. Houses provide a sense of identity and belonging, and the inter-house competitions — in sport, drama, music, quiz, and more — are keenly contested throughout the year.

Personal Tutors

Each pupil has a personal tutor who meets with them regularly to monitor their academic progress, discuss any concerns, and provide guidance and support. Tutors are the first point of contact for parents and serve as the pupil's advocate within the school.

Wellbeing Programme

Our dedicated wellbeing team includes a school counsellor, an educational psychologist, and trained mental health first-aiders. We run a comprehensive PSHE programme covering topics such as resilience, healthy relationships, online safety, and study skills.

Peer Support

Trained Sixth Form mentors provide additional support to younger pupils, offering a friendly ear and practical advice. Our anti-bullying ambassadors help to promote a culture of kindness and respect throughout the school.

"What sets Alleyn's apart is the genuine warmth of the community. Every teacher knows every child by name, and the pastoral care is truly exceptional. Our son feels completely at home."

— Parent of Year 8 pupil

Trips & Expeditions

Learning at Alleyn's doesn't stop at the school gates. We run an extensive programme of trips and expeditions throughout the year, both in the UK and overseas.

UK Trips

Regular visits to museums, galleries, theatres, and field study centres enhance the curriculum and bring subjects to life. Residential trips include geography fieldwork in Devon, biology fieldwork in Snowdonia, and history visits to the World War I battlefields.

International Expeditions

Recent overseas trips have included language exchanges in France, Germany, and Spain; a classics trip to Greece and Italy; a sports tour to South Africa; and adventure expeditions to Nepal and Borneo. Our biennial ski trip to the Alps is always oversubscribed.

World Challenge

Sixth Form pupils have the opportunity to participate in World Challenge expeditions to destinations in Asia, Africa, and South America. These life-changing trips combine trekking, community service, and cultural immersion.
