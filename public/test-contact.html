Find Us

Alleyn's School is located in the heart of Dulwich, South London, with excellent transport links.

🗺
Alleyn's School — Townley Road, Dulwich, London SE22 8SU
Interactive map placeholder

By Train

North Dulwich station is a 10-minute walk from the school. Trains run frequently from London Bridge (15 minutes) and London Victoria (20 minutes) via the Southern Rail network.

By Bus

Several bus routes serve the Dulwich area, including the P4, 37, 40, 176, and 185. The nearest bus stop is on Townley Road, directly outside the school gates.

By Car

The school is easily accessible from the South Circular (A205) and A2. Limited visitor parking is available on site by prior arrangement. Pay-and-display parking is available on surrounding streets. We encourage the use of public transport where possible.

By Bicycle

Secure bicycle parking is available for visitors near the main entrance. Dulwich is well connected to London's cycling network.
